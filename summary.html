Challenges & Future Research

**Challenges and Future Research in Agricultural Web Applications**
**Challenges:**
1. **Limited Internet Access** – Many farmers in rural areas struggle with poor connectivity, limiting their access to online tools. 2. **User Adoption and Training** – Low digital literacy among small-scale farmers affects the effective use of web applications. 3. **Data Accuracy** – Inconsistent or incorrect data entry reduces the reliability of farming recommendations. 4. **IoT Integration** – Many farmers lack access to IoT devices, restricting precision farming capabilities. 5. **Financial Barriers** – High costs for premium features or hardware make advanced agricultural solutions inaccessible. 6. **Security and Privacy** – Farmers need assurance that their data is protected from unauthorized access. **Future Research Directions:**
1. **AI-Powered Crop Management** – Implementing AI-driven insights for smarter farming decisions. 2. **Offline Accessibility** – Developing offline features to support farmers in low-connectivity areas. 3. **Farmer Education Programs** – Enhancing digital literacy through in-app tutorials and training. 4. **Blockchain for Transparency** – Ensuring fair pricing and secure transactions in agricultural trade. 5. **Mobile Optimization** – Improving mobile-friendly interfaces for better usability. 6. **Multi-Language Support** – Offering local language options to increase accessibility for diverse farmers. .
